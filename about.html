Apa kabar! I'm Michelle, a Chinese-Indonesian Product and Visual Designer based in Vancouver, BC. As a designer, I'm deeply interested in the intersection of my passion for Art Direction and Experience Design, striving to craft designs that not only push boundaries but also honor the design principles and the contextual constraints. Through my background, my designs are inherently driven by the pursuit of seeking emotions and crafting narratives that resonates to the user.

Outside of design, I find joy in creating tableware in pottery, capturing random moments with my analog camera, and exploring new eateries and coffee shops around the city with my friends.
